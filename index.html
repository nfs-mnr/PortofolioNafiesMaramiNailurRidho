About Me

Hello, I am Nafies Marami Nailur Ridho, a Telecommunications Engineering student at Telkom University and a Satellite Enthusiast focused on Radio Frequency (RF) Engineering. Currently, I am an active Research Assistant at the Nano Satellite Laboratory, where I am directly involved in the development and fabrication of pico-satellites, specializing in the antenna and RF systems division.

In my research, I have a deep interest in antenna design, link budget analysis, and transmission parameter modeling. I am experienced in utilizing CST Studio Suite for design simulation and performing hands-on hardware testing using a Vector Network Analyzer (VNA), NanoVNA, and Spectrum Analyzer to ensure optimal system performance.

I believe that precise RF engineering is the key to building reliable and efficient satellite communication systems. Through my technical experience and dedication at the laboratory, I am committed to developing innovative communication solutions in the field of satellite technology.
